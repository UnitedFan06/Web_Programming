Movie Summmaries:

Moneyball
A baseball manager, Billy Beane, revolutionizes the sport by using data analytics and unconventional strategies to build a competitive team on a limited budget. Despite initial skepticism and failures, the Oakland A's achieve unprecedented success. The movie highlights the power of innovation, determination, and challenging traditional norms.
Interstellar
A group of astronauts ventures into space to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. The story explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the mysteries of time and space through the bond between a father and his daughter. Christopher Nolan delivers a visually stunning, emotionally charged tale of humanity's survival.
The Social Network
Mark Zuckerberg's journey from a Harvard student to the co-founder of Facebook reveals the creation and rise of the social media giant. The film delves into the complexities of ambition, innovation, and the personal betrayals that accompany massive success. It paints a dramatic portrait of the challenges and controversies behind one of the world's most influential platforms.
